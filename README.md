# Dealer

This project aims to do a few things simultaneously.

## Poker

Mainly involving statistics and strategy for Poker, initially Texas Hold'em.

### Gameplay engine

First step of which, of course, is to implement the rules of the game.

### Data and Statistics

The next of which will include the study of hands. This aims to provide a general understanding of the relative strengths of various hands over the course of the game. Doing this is intended to provide a better intuition regarding the orders of magnitude involved during these calculations and portray the design decisions made in order to support increasing requirements in real-world use-cases.

### Decision Making

This project also endeavors to provide a demonstration of developing meta-game conditions that lead to evolving strategies. This involves the recreation of prevailing modern strategies (over the range of skill levels), the methods of exploitation applicable to developed strategies, and the understanding of data construction/acquisition/relevance.
